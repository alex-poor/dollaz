# Analysis & projections

**By category over time** plots your biggest categories month by month, so you can see what's trending.

**Projection** forecasts a chosen category (or the total) forward:

- **Average** — holds flat at the average of the last 3 months.
- **Trend** — extrapolates the straight-line trend, clamped at zero.

Pick a horizon (3 / 6 / 12 months). The shaded region of the chart is projected.

The **per-category outlook** table shows each category's recent monthly average, whether it's rising or easing, and the projected next-month and full-horizon totals.

Projections are a rough planning guide based only on your own history — not financial advice.
